Critique your two prototypes

  1. Prepare your two prototypes to share
    • What are the critical elements of the design's value?
    • How will you explain these critical elements?
    • What kind of feedback will you ask for?
  2. Get feedback from your sister team.
  3. Discuss your feedback with your partner.
    • Were your ideas useful to them?
    • Was one of the ideas better? Why?
    • Can you think of better ideas based on what you learned about these ideas?
  4. Narrow down to one idea.
    • How will you prototype this to best explain its value?
    • What fidelity is necessary to make it's functionality clear?
