Critique

Materials: 1 piece of paper for each team for critique notes

Find your partner (1 min)

Open your InVision prototype, or your paper prototype if you don't have one.

Find two pairs to join (3 min)

You'll each critique each other group's design.

Critique designs (30 min)

Take 10 minutes each to critique each pair's design.

  • Ask every question you can think of about every detail, uncovering rationale.
  • If you're making a judgement, use the hamburger method.
  • Keep notes on the critiques to practice capturing critiques while listening (you'll also turn these in).

Reflection (10 min)

  • What was difficult about giving critique?
  • What was difficult about receiving critique?
  • Were there things you feel you could not say during critique?
  • What are the limitations of this method of evaluation?

Plan revisions (10 min)

Now that you have feedback on your designs, synthesize it into a written plan with your partner.

  • What will you change about your design?
  • What will you not change about your design?
  • What questions do you still have about your design that demand more insight?
  • What prototype could you create to answer these questions and how would you use the prototype to answer them?

Critique plans (5 min)

Describe your plans to the class. Let's deconstruct what's good about your plan and what could be better.
